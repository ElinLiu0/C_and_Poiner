## C中变量的最小范围
| 类型    | 最小范围 |
|----|---|
|  char  |0~127|
|  singed char  |-127 ~ 127|
|  unsigned char  | 0~255|
| short int| -32768 ~ 32767|
| unsigned short int| 0 ~ 65535|
| int| -32767 ~ 32767|
| unsigned int| 0 ~ 65535|
| long int| -2147483648 ~ 2147483647|
| unsigned long int| 0 ~ 4294967295|
